What's New in IPA

Updated Friday, September 22, 2017

New

  • Ability to mark 'The Staff' and 'AI Placeholder' when assigning instructors

  • Auto-logout time will be increased from one to four hours

  • Work-life balance is an option in the Teaching Call form and Assign Instructors page

  • New pre-set (standard) time options in the Scheduling View: 50-minute and 3-hour once-a-week options

  • A warning is shown when smartphones and tablets attempt to load IPA

Fixed

  • Duplicate, non-clickable activities sometimes appear in the Scheduling View

  • Activity calendar room filter will stop showing related activities not found in the filtered rooms

  • Miscellaneous small bugs in the Support Call module

  • Support Staff tab preference assignment functionality restored
